# Percentage Decrease Calculator

A **Percentage Decrease Calculator** is a simple tool that helps you find how much a value has decreased compared to its original value. Percentage decrease is commonly used in daily life, such as calculating shopping discounts, tracking business losses, or comparing changes in numbers.

Instead of performing calculations manually, you can quickly find the result using an online **[Percentage Decrease Calculator](https://percentagedecreasecalculator.com/)**. Simply enter the original value and the new value, and the calculator will instantly show the percentage decrease. This saves time and helps avoid mistakes.

---

## What Is Percentage Decrease?

Percentage decrease shows **how much a value has reduced compared to its starting value**. It expresses the reduction as a percentage.

Examples include:

- A phone price drops from ₹20,000 to ₹16,000  
- A company’s revenue decreases from $10,000 to $8,000  
- A product price is reduced during a sale  

In these situations, percentage decrease helps measure **how large the reduction is relative to the original value**.
